2 Mins Maggi for a rainy day

Hello Foodies! Today I will be sharing a quick, savory instant noodles recipe for a rainy day. It will only take 2 minn of your time and will fill your stomach for the rest off the night

Ingredients

  • Maggi packet -2
  • oil - 1tbsp
  • minced garlic - 2 cloves
  • chopped onions - 1cup
  • chopped capsicum - 1/2 cup
  • chopped carrot- 1/2 cup
  • corn - 1 cup
  • white vinegar - 1tsp
  • soy sauce - 1tsp

Instructions

  1. add oil, garlic and chilli to the pan
  2. add onionns and fry them until golden brown
  3. add the vegatables and cover it with a lid
  4. add salt, chilli powder and maggi masala
  5. add vinegar and soy sauce and mix it
  6. add water and noodles and bring it to boil
  7. once the maggi is cooked serve it in a bowl
  8. garnish with some spring onions

Your maggi is ready!
