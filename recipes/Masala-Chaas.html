Masala Chaas for hot summers

Masala chaas is one summer drink which you can made even in advance. Make it ahead and keep it refrigerated till ready to serve. It tastes best when chilled. Stay cool and enjoy this recipe! xx

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 cups water
  • chaat masala - 1tsp
  • ground pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • green chillies - 2
  • salt - 2 tsp
  • corriander leaves- 15 leaves

Instructions

  1. add all the ingredients to the blender
  2. blend them all and transfer it into a differnt pot
  3. store it in the fridge for an hour
  4. serve in glasses

Enjoy your Masala-chaas!
